    Our courses cater to a wide range of learners, from beginners to experienced individuals looking to enhance their Python and Data Science skills. Python 02: Python for Data Science is designed for those who have completed Python 01 or have prior Python knowledge.

    Yes, we offer a certificate of completion for both Python 01 and Python 02 courses. These certificates can be a valuable addition to your resume or portfolio.
